Services

Mission Medics is a newly established nonprofit dedicated to expanding access to essential medical equipment and life-saving resources for individuals and families in need. Serving communities across Frisco, Texas, we support people of all ages living with illness, injury, or disability. We carry out our mission through three core service areas: Medical Equipment Redistribution, Emergency Medical Support, and Community Health Assistance.

Through Medical Equipment Reuse, Mission Medics collects donations of gently used medical equipment from hospitals, clinics, partner organizations, and community members. Each item is carefully evaluated to ensure it is safe and functional before being professionally refurbished, sanitized to state health standards, repaired if necessary, and recorded into inventory. Once approved for use, the equipment is distributed to individuals and families in need at no cost or low cost.
